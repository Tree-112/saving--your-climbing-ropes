Saving Your Climbing: Unknown Factors That Can Degrade Your Rope and How to Mitigate It

Climbing ropes can fail long before any damage is visible, worn down by UV exposure, moisture, and repeated use. This site breaks down the science behind rope degradation and explores emerging features, like hydrophobic coatings, that could make climbing gear safer. Explore the research, a short video, and the studies behind it.
